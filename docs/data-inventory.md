Bedrock processes active prompts and approved prose to provide the feature. Production cannot proceed until the workload retention and model-invocation-logging posture is independently reviewed and recorded using the preflight checklist.

Staff passwords, TOTP codes, reset codes, Cognito challenge sessions, and Cognito tokens are never written to application storage or logs. They exist transiently in the staff browser, Lambda memory, and Cognito processing. The browser receives challenge state only inside an authenticated-encrypted, expiring transaction.
